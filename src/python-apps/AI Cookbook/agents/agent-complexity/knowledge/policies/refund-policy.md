# Refund Policy

## Duplicate Charges
- Automatic refund for duplicate charges under $100
- Refunds over $100 require manager approval (L2 review)
- Must be reported within 30 days of the charge date
- Refund processing time: 3-5 business days

## Subscription Cancellation
- Pro-rated refund for annual plans cancelled within 14 days
- Monthly plans: no refund for current billing period
- Downgrade takes effect at next billing cycle

## Exceptions
- Promotional pricing is non-refundable
- Enterprise contracts follow custom terms
