# Subscription Management

## Plans
| Plan | Price | Features |
|------|-------|----------|
| Starter | $9.99/mo | Basic features, 1 user |
| Professional | $49.99/mo | All features, 5 users, priority support |
| Enterprise | Custom | Unlimited users, dedicated account manager, SLA |

## Modifications
- Upgrades take effect immediately
- Downgrades take effect at next billing cycle
- Plan changes mid-cycle are pro-rated for upgrades only

## Billing
- All plans billed on the 1st of each month
- Failed payments retried on day 3, 7, and 14
- Account suspended after 3 failed payment attempts
